What Siri AI is great at — and where Super goes further

Siri AI

Siri AI is a voice‑first assistant rebuilt around deep local context. Apple indexes messages, email, calendars, and other on‑device data so many requests are handled privately without sending raw personal data to the cloud.

  • Excellent personal context on Apple devices
  • Strong privacy via local‑first processing
  • Tightly integrated with iOS, macOS, and iCloud

Super

Super is built as a personal AI agent that actually operates a computer — clicking, typing, navigating, and finishing multi‑step workflows. Its defining advantage is a reusable computer-use cache, so repeated tasks don’t start from scratch every time.

  • Real computer operation, not just voice commands
  • Cache reuse for repeated workflows
  • Better fit for ongoing operational work

Why computer‑use agents matter now

Local context vs execution

Hands‑on testing shows Siri AI excels at understanding personal data across Messages, Mail, and Calendar — often outperforming cloud assistants like Gemini in those scenarios.

System orchestration has limits

Siri can suggest, draft, and schedule — but it doesn’t act as a durable computer‑use worker that learns and replays long operational workflows.

Durable workflows compound

For repeated computer tasks — internal tools, dashboards, admin panels — Super’s cache means the second, tenth, and hundredth run can be cheaper and faster than the first.
